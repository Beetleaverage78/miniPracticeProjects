Our Initiative


The goal of this company is to provide Kiwis easy access to agricultural products all across New Zealand. After all, agriculture is NZ's biggest industry. providing about 70% of the countries total earnings in exports alone.

We also believe in providing our local farmers an opportunity to expand their company, by being the bridge to their international customers, and 100% of the profits will go directly to our suppliers.

This is because we are a Government Funded Project, and we simply exist to provide a platform, so you can buy fresh products from the comfort of your home.
